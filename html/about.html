About Me

Jenny Stewart

I am a student at BYU, studying Experience Design and Management. As a working professional and student, I am excited for what the future holds. With the experience that I have had and the knowledge that I am gaining in my classes, I am ready to take the field and transform businesses. Experience design is something that is often overlooked or misunderstood but it is the key element to a successful business. All businesses rely on their customers and the experiences they have. As working professionals, it is our responsibility to provide the best experience to our customers. I am ready to help you help your customers. I know what works and have the skills and energy required to turn your business into a great one. I am intuitive and I am constantly seeking out new ways to transform experiences. I love getting to know people and finding ways to serve them and I can't wait to do the same with your customers.
